## o3de_mascot.png

The Open 3D Engine mascot ("Odie"), used as the default character sprite across
the Diorama demos (the side-scroller player and props, the lighting/materials
subjects, the camera-follow target, and the hello-sprite intro).

- Source: <https://o3de.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/o3de_mascot_color-797x1024.png>
- Owner: the Open 3D Engine project / The Linux Foundation.
- Use: this is O3DE project branding included in an O3DE community gem's samples.
  It is the property of the O3DE project and is used here under O3DE's branding
  guidelines (<https://o3de.org/brand-guidelines/>), not under this repository's
  license. Follow those guidelines before reusing or redistributing it.
