# Texture attribution

Most textures here are generated placeholders (see `_gen_textures.py`) and are
covered by this repository's license. Third-party assets are listed below.

## o3de_mascot.png

The Open 3D Engine mascot ("Odie"), used as the default character sprite across
the Diorama demos (the side-scroller player and props, the lighting/materials
subjects, the camera-follow target, and the hello-sprite intro). Odie is the
official community-selected O3DE mascot (created by Steve Pham, inspired by the
shader ball that appears in every default level); see the introduction at
<https://o3de.org/meet-odie/>.

- Source: <https://o3de.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/o3de_mascot_color-797x1024.png>
- Owner: the Open 3D Engine project / The Linux Foundation.
- Use: this is O3DE project branding included in an O3DE community gem's samples.
  It is the property of the O3DE project and is used here under O3DE's branding
  guidelines (<https://o3de.org/brand-guidelines/>), not under this repository's
  license. Follow those guidelines before reusing or redistributing it.

## octopus.png ("Obi")

The twin-stick player. A generic procedurally-drawn cartoon octopus generated by
`_gen_textures.py` (and composed into `keyart/obi-and-the-haters.svg` by
`_gen_poster_svg.py`). It is original art with no specific palette or signature
features, covered by this repository's license. The name "Obi" is original to
this project.
